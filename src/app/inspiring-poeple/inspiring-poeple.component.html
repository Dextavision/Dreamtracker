Dean Schneider

Dean Schneider is born in switzerland and he LOVES animals. Once he was a pretty successfully entrepreneur and had his own financial company. He had everything that was purchasable but his real passion are animals. In 2017 he moved to south africa and established a wildlife sanctuary and rehabilitation center with the wonderful name Hakuna Mipaka (stands for "no limits" in Swahili).
Dean is a 100% positive human and always carry a smile on his face wherever he goes. Also he is the perfect example that money cant buy you happiness. Always chase your passion and your dreams!

His dream? Bring animals into poeples hearts.
