What is Dreamtracker?


What is the idea behind Dreamtracker?

Im just like you. Well, at least i think so. I work everyday in a nine to five job. On top of that i need round about 2,5 hours to get to my job and back home. Everyday. This is so much time. And within this time you dont work for yourself. You have a boss who is chasing his dreams. And when you come back home you are stressed and tired and just want to relax. I have reached the point where a voice in my head said to me: No, this is not enough for me. I want more for myself!

This is the birth of Dreamtracker!


Now im chasing my dreams. After work i work hard for my goals even if its only 5 minutes. Every minute is important. I started to write my dreams down and divided it into multiple goals. You need to bring your goals to paper, or in my case to the E-Paper. You need to see them. Make yourself a to-do list and start working on this every single day. Dreamtracker will help you with chasing your dreams.

Write your dreams and goals down.

Divide your dreams in goals.

Make yourself a to-do list.

Connect with other people who are chasing their dreams.




Dreams are worth chasing!
